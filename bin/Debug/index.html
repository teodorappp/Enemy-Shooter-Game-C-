Year 2263

In the vast expanse of space, humanity faces its greatest threat yet - an alien armada known as the Xerions. As they sweep through the galaxy, leaving destruction in their wake, only one thing stands between them and the annihilation of Earth: the Prime Target Squadron.

At the helm of this elite squadron is Captain Alex Turner, a skilled pilot with nerves of steel and a heart of courage. With his trusty starfighter, the Phoenix, he leads the charge against the Xerion forces, engaging them in epic dogfights across the cosmos.
